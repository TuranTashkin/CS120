Our story

The Last Train formed in 2018 when four friends from Malden decided to bring high-energy pop and rock covers to weddings, corporate parties, and backyard bashes. We've played over 150 shows across New England, from intimate clubs to festival stages. Our motto: “every song is a one-way ticket to a good time.”

We pride ourselves on reading the room — whether it's 80s rock, modern pop, or soulful ballads, we tailor each set to make your event unforgettable.
